Meet the Team:



Cristela Rochell - President

Cristela Rochell is the current President of the Alpha Phi Psi chapter of Sigma Tau Delta. As well as being involved in literary endeavors on campus, she is a leader in UNC's Air Force ROTC. She will graduate in May of 2018 and plans to join the Air Force.


Julia Whitten - Treasurer

Julia Whitten is the current Treasurer of the Alpha Phi Psi chapter of Sigma Tau Delta. She is a Junior at UNC, majoring in English, and she's teaching her own class this semester. Next year, Julia will continue to be involved in Sigma Tau Delta as the chapter's President.


Tessa Palmer - Public Relations Officer

Tessa Palmer is currently the Public Relations Officer of the Alpha Phi Psi chapter of Sigma Tau Delta. She is majoring in English and minoring in Composition, Rhetoric, and Digital Literacy. She has no idea what she's doing in the future, AKA the Endless Void.
