Eligibility and Benefits

  • Membership Requirements

    Undergraduate and graduate students are eligible and need not be majoring or minoring in the discipline of English. Interested applicants should have:

      • 1. Completed a minimum of two college courses in English language or literature beyond the usual requirements in freshman English
      • 2. A minimum of a B or equivalent average in English
      • 3. Read the rights of student membership

  • Membership Cost

    Each new member pays a one-time international induction fee of $40 for a lifetime membership. The UNC Chapter does not charge dues.

  • Membership Benefits

      • 1. Academic Recognition
      • 2. Scholarships and Internships
      • 3. Writing Awards and Opporuntities
      • 4. Annual Convention
      • 5. Student Leadership and Community Service
      • 6. Career Advancement
      • 7. Lifetime Affiliation and Fellowship
